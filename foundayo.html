Important Safety Information

Foundayo® (orforglipron) is a prescription medication indicated as an adjunct to diet and exercise for chronic weight management. Common side effects include nausea, diarrhea, vomiting, decreased appetite, constipation, and abdominal pain. Foundayo should not be used in patients with a personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or Multiple Endocrine Neoplasia syndrome type 2.

Protected Health Information

Certain information collected or processed through the Service may constitute Protected Health Information ("PHI") as defined under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 ("HIPAA") and applicable state laws.

Clyr Health, LLC is not a healthcare provider and does not independently provide medical care. However, in certain circumstances, Clyr Health may receive, maintain, transmit, or process PHI on behalf of affiliated medical providers, medical groups, or pharmacies. In those circumstances, Clyr Health acts as a Business Associate under HIPAA and handles PHI in accordance with applicable Business Associate Agreements ("BAAs"), HIPAA regulations, and applicable state laws.

PHI may include individually identifiable information relating to an individual's health condition, healthcare services, or payment for healthcare services, whether past, present, or future, when such information is created, received, maintained, or transmitted on behalf of a covered entity.

Information that has been properly de-identified in accordance with HIPAA standards is not considered PHI and is not subject to HIPAA requirements.

Where Clyr Health processes PHI as a Business Associate, the use and disclosure of that PHI is governed by applicable BAAs and the Notice of Privacy Practices of the applicable healthcare provider or pharmacy, not by this Privacy Policy except as required by law.

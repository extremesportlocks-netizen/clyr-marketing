Frequently Asked

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about starting your GLP-1 weight loss journey with CLYR.

Compounded Semaglutide is a custom-prepared medication made by licensed 503A compounding pharmacies. It is prescribed by a licensed healthcare provider and combined with Vitamin B12 to support your weight management journey. Compounded medications are not FDA-approved.

Semaglutide targets the GLP-1 receptor, helping regulate appetite and blood sugar. Tirzepatide targets both GLP-1 and GIP receptors (dual-action), which may provide enhanced weight loss results. Your provider will recommend the best option based on your health profile and goals.

Most patients begin noticing reduced appetite within the first 1–2 weeks. Changes typically build over the first few months of consistent, provider-supervised treatment. Individual results vary based on dosing, diet, and activity level.

Every CLYR patient is evaluated by a licensed medical provider via our telehealth platform before receiving a prescription. Our providers review your health history, current medications, and goals to determine if treatment is appropriate for you.

If you cancel during your current 90-day period, you will not be charged for the next renewal. If you're deemed ineligible by your provider, we offer a full refund.

CLYR does not bill insurance. We are a cash-pay service, and you can download itemized receipts and your full order history from the patient portal at any time.

Our services are available in all 50 states.

Our compounding pharmacy may occasionally substitute Vitamin B6 (Pyridoxine) for B12 (Methylcobalamin) based on ingredient availability. Both vitamins support energy and metabolism during your weight loss journey. Your medication's active ingredient, Tirzepatide or Semaglutide, remains exactly the same regardless of which vitamin is included. You will always be notified if there is any change to your prescription.

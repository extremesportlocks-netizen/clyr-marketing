Frequently Asked

Questions about
NAD+?

NAD+ (nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide) is a coenzyme found in every living cell. It plays a critical role in energy metabolism, DNA repair, and cellular signaling. NAD+ levels naturally decline with age.

Injections bypass the digestive system, delivering NAD+ directly to your bloodstream for faster absorption and higher bioavailability. Oral supplements are broken down during digestion.

Many patients report improved energy and mental clarity within 1-2 weeks. Sleep and mood improvements follow within 2-4 weeks. Long-term cellular benefits develop over months.

NAD+ injections are generally well tolerated. The most common side effect is mild injection site discomfort. Consult your provider if you have specific health conditions or take other medications.

Dosage and frequency are determined by your provider based on your goals. Typical protocols range from 2-3 times per week.

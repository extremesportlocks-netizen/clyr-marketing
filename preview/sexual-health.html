Important Safety Information

These treatments are prescription medications provided only after review by a licensed provider; not everyone qualifies. PDE5 inhibitors (such as sildenafil and tadalafil) must never be combined with nitrate medications and are not appropriate for men with certain cardiovascular conditions. Seek emergency care for an erection lasting more than four hours. PT-141 can transiently raise blood pressure. Some treatments are compounded; compounded medications are not FDA-approved and are not reviewed by the FDA for safety, efficacy, or quality before reaching patients. Tell your provider about all medications and conditions. Individual results vary.

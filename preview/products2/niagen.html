The Details

What you are actually getting.

Why Niagen

Niagen is the branded, patented form of nicotinamide riboside (NR), a B3-family molecule the body converts into NAD+, the coenzyme tied to cellular energy and the repair enzymes associated with healthy aging. NAD+ levels tend to decline with age, which is why precursors like NR have drawn interest. Human research is still developing.

Why injectable

Oral forms are broken down and diluted before much reaches the bloodstream. The injectable route is designed to bypass that limit. If you prefer the coenzyme itself, we also offer NAD+, and your provider can help you choose.

Important Safety Information

Niagen (nicotinamide riboside) injections are a prescription wellness treatment provided only after review by a licensed provider; not everyone qualifies. This product is compounded; compounded medications are not FDA-approved and are not reviewed by the FDA for safety, efficacy, or quality before reaching patients. Rapid administration can cause transient flushing, warmth, or nausea, so it is given slowly. Avoid during pregnancy or breastfeeding unless your provider advises otherwise. Tell your provider about all medications and conditions. Individual results vary.

Important Safety Information

This is a prescription medication provided only after review by a licensed provider; not everyone qualifies. PDE5 inhibitors must never be combined with nitrate medications and are not appropriate for men with certain cardiovascular conditions. Seek emergency care for an erection lasting more than four hours. Some products are compounded; compounded medications are not FDA-approved and are not reviewed by the FDA for safety, efficacy, or quality before reaching patients. Tell your provider about all medications and conditions. Individual results vary.

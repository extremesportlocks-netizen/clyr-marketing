The Details

What you are actually getting.

Why NAD+

NAD+ is a coenzyme present in every cell, central to converting food into energy and to the repair enzymes associated with healthy aging. Levels tend to decline with age, which is why supplementation has drawn interest.

Why injectable

Oral NAD+ is broken down before much reaches the bloodstream. The injectable route is designed to bypass that limit. We also offer the precursor, Niagen, if you prefer.

Important Safety Information

GLP-1 medications are prescription treatments provided only after review by a licensed provider; not everyone qualifies. They should not be used by individuals with a personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or multiple endocrine neoplasia syndrome type 2, and carry risks including pancreatitis, gallbladder problems, and gastrointestinal effects. Not for use in pregnancy. Some options are compounded; compounded medications are not FDA-approved and are not reviewed by the FDA for safety, efficacy, or quality before reaching patients. Tell your provider about all medications and conditions. Individual results vary.

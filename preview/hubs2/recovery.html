Important Safety Information

These are prescription treatments provided only after provider review. Low-dose naltrexone is used off-label; naltrexone blocks opioids and must not be used by anyone taking opioid medications or dependent on opioids, or in acute hepatitis or liver failure. Recovery peptides are research-stage with limited human data and should be avoided in pregnancy, breastfeeding, or with active cancer. Compounded medications are not FDA-approved and are not reviewed by the FDA for safety, efficacy, or quality before reaching patients. Tell your provider about all medications and conditions. Individual results vary.

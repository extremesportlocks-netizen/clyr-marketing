Important Safety Information

Hormone therapy is a prescription treatment provided only after provider review; it is not appropriate for everyone, including those with a history of estrogen-sensitive cancers, blood clots, stroke, or active liver disease, or during pregnancy. If you have a uterus, estrogen is generally combined with a progestogen. Report any unusual vaginal bleeding to your provider. Some treatments are compounded; compounded medications are not FDA-approved and are not reviewed by the FDA for safety, efficacy, or quality before reaching patients. Tell your provider about all medications and conditions. Individual results vary.

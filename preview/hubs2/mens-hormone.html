Important Safety Information

Testosterone therapy is a prescription, controlled treatment for men with diagnosed low testosterone; it is not for women, pregnancy, or athletic enhancement, and is not appropriate for men with prostate or breast cancer. It requires ongoing lab monitoring (testosterone, hematocrit, estradiol). Topical testosterone can transfer to others through skin contact; wash hands and cover the area. Some treatments are compounded; compounded medications are not FDA-approved and are not reviewed by the FDA for safety, efficacy, or quality before reaching patients. Tell your provider about all medications and conditions. Individual results vary.

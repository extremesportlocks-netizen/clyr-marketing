Important Safety Information

These peptides are prescription, compounded treatments provided only after provider review. They are research-stage compounds; the majority of evidence is from animal studies and rigorous human safety and efficacy data is limited. Compounded medications are not FDA-approved and are not reviewed by the FDA for safety, efficacy, or quality before reaching patients. They should be avoided during pregnancy or breastfeeding and by individuals with active cancer, and may be prohibited in competitive sport. Tell your provider about all medications and conditions.

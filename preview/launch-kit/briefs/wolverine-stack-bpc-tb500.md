## 2. Market positioning

**"Wolverine Stack"** is CLYR's branded name for the common clinic pattern of combining BPC-157 and TB-500 in recovery protocols. The combo targets patients who want broader coverage than either peptide alone:

- **BPC-157:** Gastric-derived pentadecapeptide; richer tendon/gut preclinical literature; often dosed daily subQ (250–500 mcg typical in market).
- **TB-500:** Thymosin beta-4 fragment; actin/migration framing; often pulsed 2x/week (2.5 mg typical in market).

**Competitive anchor:** Peptide combo sellers $200–300/mo gray market; CLYR differentiator = LegitScript telehealth + 503A pharmacy + provider supervision.

**Evidence framing (required):** Preclinical interest is strong; human RCT evidence for either agent or the combo is limited. Not FDA-approved. WADA-prohibited for competitive athletes. Educational marketing only until regulatory GO.

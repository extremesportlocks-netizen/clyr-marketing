## What the preview work is (recap)

Two threads live under `/preview/`:

**1. Dark / premium "hot" redesign (the main effort).** A full re-skin of the marketing site to a cinematic dark standard — ember + teal glow, confident "real talk" voice, tagged product grids, how-it-works, straight FAQ, ISI on every medical page. ~35 core pages built; lives entirely in preview, live site untouched, awaiting go-live OK.
- **Master template / vibe:** `preview/sexual-health.html`
- **Shared stylesheet:** `preview/assets/clyr-vibe.css` (per-vertical accent set via inline `<body>` `--accent` / `--hero-glow-1/2`)
- **Dark hubs:** `preview/hubs2/` · **dark product pages:** `preview/products2/` (image-free, generated by `scripts/build-dark-product-pages.py`)
- **Compliance baked in:** noindex/nofollow, no em dashes, no efficacy claims (no %/cure/guaranteed/clinically-proven), no competitor-brand comparisons, provider + ISI on medical pages.

**2. Homepage v2 mockup (`/preview/` + version sets).** A from-scratch homepage explored, then reverted on the live site — preserved here for a surgical merge (credo pull-quote, 12-product wall, comparison table, finale CTA). Details in `preview/HANDOFF.md`.

What to expect

A realistic timeline.

Hair regrowth is slow. The follicles need time to wake up. Most patients see meaningful results by month 6, with continued improvement through month 12.

Month 1 to 2

Initial shedding

Some patients experience a temporary shed as old hairs are pushed out by new growth. This is a normal sign the treatment is working.

Month 3 to 4

Shed stabilizes

The early shed subsides. Hair loss slows. Some patients begin to notice less hair in the shower and on the pillow.

Month 6

Visible regrowth

New baby hairs become visible along the hairline and crown. Most patients describe their hair as fuller. Photo progress becomes clear.

Month 12

Full results

Maximum density typically reached around the 12 month mark. Continued treatment maintains results. Stopping causes the hair gained to be lost again over 6 to 12 months.

Important Safety Information

Tadalafil and Vardenafil are prescription PDE5 inhibitors. Common side effects include headache, flushing, nasal congestion, back pain, and indigestion. Do not take with nitrates or alpha-blockers. Seek immediate medical attention for erections lasting more than 4 hours (priapism) or sudden vision/hearing changes.

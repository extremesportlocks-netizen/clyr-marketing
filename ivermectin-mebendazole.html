Not all ivermectin is
created equal.

Where you get it matters as much as whether you take it. Not every source is safe, and not every dose is right.

Amazon & social “supplements”

Sold without a pharmacy license, so there is no way to verify what is actually in the capsule or at what strength.

Overseas pharmacies

No U.S. physician oversight, unknown compounding standards, and shipments that can be seized at customs.

Veterinary “horse paste”

Made for animals, not people. No purity testing, no dose verification, and no physician involved.

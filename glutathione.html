Frequently Asked

Questions about
Glutathione?

Glutathione is a powerful antioxidant naturally produced by your body, composed of three amino acids: cysteine, glycine, and glutamine. It protects cells from oxidative stress, supports detoxification, and boosts immune function. Levels decline with age and stress.

Oral glutathione is largely broken down in the digestive tract before absorption. Injectable glutathione bypasses digestion entirely, delivering the full dose directly to your cells.

Many patients notice improved energy within 1-2 weeks. Skin improvements (brighter tone, reduced spots) may take 4-8 weeks. Immune and detox benefits build over time.

Glutathione is generally well tolerated with minimal drug interactions. Always inform your provider of all medications you take.

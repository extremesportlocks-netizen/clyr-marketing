Important Safety Information

Wegovy® (semaglutide) tablets are a prescription medication for chronic weight management. Contains a boxed warning about thyroid C-cell tumors. Contraindicated in patients with personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or MEN 2. Common side effects include nausea, diarrhea, vomiting, constipation, and abdominal pain.

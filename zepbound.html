Important Safety Information

Zepbound® (tirzepatide) is a prescription medication. It is indicated as an adjunct to a reduced calorie diet and increased physical activity for chronic weight management in adults with an initial BMI of 30 kg/m² or greater, or 27 kg/m² or greater with at least one weight-related comorbid condition. Zepbound contains a boxed warning regarding the risk of thyroid C-cell tumors. It is contraindicated in patients with a personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or Multiple Endocrine Neoplasia syndrome type 2. Common side effects include nausea, diarrhea, decreased appetite, vomiting, constipation, and abdominal pain. For full prescribing information, talk to your provider or visit the Eli Lilly website.

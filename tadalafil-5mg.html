Important Safety Information

Tadalafil is a prescription PDE5 inhibitor. Do not take tadalafil if you use nitrates (medicines for chest pain) in any form, the combination can cause a serious drop in blood pressure. Use caution with alpha-blockers or other blood-pressure medications; your physician will screen for interactions. Common side effects include headache, flushing, indigestion, back pain, and muscle aches. Seek immediate medical attention for an erection lasting more than 4 hours (priapism) or any sudden decrease or loss of vision or hearing.
